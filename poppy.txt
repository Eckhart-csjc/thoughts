Stumbling mindlessly
  (or mind-elsewherely)
    feet on the sidewalk
    head in the nowhere world
    of terraform, LLMs and deadlines

Suddenly an orange flame arrests my thoughts
  and rests my thought
    on a poppy growing through a crack
    cheerfully resisting concrete intentions

Yours is not an invasive species, little one --
  mine is.
  grow in peace.
